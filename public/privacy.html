Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information KashBook collects, why we collect it, who we share it with, how we protect it, and the rights you have. KashBook is operated by Reliant Technology Solutions and processes personal data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.

1. Who We Are & Scope

This policy explains what personal data KashBook collects, why, who we share it with, and your rights. We process personal data in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.

2. Information We Collect

Account details: your name, phone number, email, and business name. Business records: the transactions, customers, inventory, and invoices you enter. Verification (KYC) data when you open a bank account: BVN, date of birth, gender, business registration (CAC) number and documents, business address, and for limited companies the names, BVNs, and details of directors and shareholders. Banking data: your account number, balances, transfers, and saved transfer recipients. Device data: a push-notification token if you allow notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

To run your bookkeeping, operate your bank account and transfers, verify your identity as required by law, prevent fraud and money laundering, send you service notifications (like payment alerts and your daily business summary), and improve the app. We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their marketing.

4. Identity Verification & Banking Partners

When you apply for a bank account, your verification data is shared with our CBN-licensed banking partner, which issues your account, and with a licensed identity-verification provider that checks your BVN and CAC details against official records. Verification lookups are kept for a maximum of 24 hours before being purged; only an irreversible fingerprint is retained to prevent duplicate registrations.

5. Service Providers

We use vetted providers to deliver parts of the service: SMS providers for verification codes, an email provider for receipts and codes, a media service for profile and document images, app-store billing for subscriptions, and push-notification delivery. Each receives only the minimum data needed for its job.

6. How We Protect Your Data

All traffic is encrypted in transit (TLS). Sensitive identifiers like your BVN and CAC number are additionally encrypted at rest with AES-256 and never appear in system logs. Your transaction PIN is stored only as a one-way hash. We recommend protecting your device with a screen lock.

7. Biometrics

Face ID and fingerprint unlock are processed entirely by your device’s operating system. KashBook never sees, stores, or transmits your biometric data — your device simply tells the app whether the check passed.

8. Legal & Compliance Obligations

As a financial app we are required to monitor transactions for suspicious activity, keep audit records, and cooperate with our banking partner and regulators. We may retain and disclose records where required by Nigerian law (for example anti-money-laundering rules), even after an account is closed.

9. How Long We Keep Data

Business records are kept while your account is active. Identity-verification lookups are purged within 24 hours. Compliance and transaction audit records are retained for the period required by law (typically five years) after which they are deleted.

10. Notifications

With your permission we send push notifications for payments received, transfer confirmations, account updates, and a daily business summary around 8pm. You can turn notifications off in Profile → Settings or in your device settings at any time.

11. Contacts Permission

KashBook requests access to your phone contacts only when you choose to add a customer using the phonebook picker. We read contact names and numbers solely to fill the customer form — we do not store, upload, or share your contact list.

12. Sharing Receipts & Invoices

When you use Share features, receipt or invoice content is passed to the third-party app you choose (WhatsApp, SMS, email). KashBook does not control how those apps handle the shared content.

13. Instagram Integration (Meta Platform)

If you choose to connect your Instagram professional account, KashBook uses the Instagram API with Instagram Login so you can read and reply to your own customers’ direct messages, and send your bank-transfer payment details, from inside the app. With your explicit authorization we access only: (a) your Instagram account ID and username, to identify the connected account; and (b) the direct messages exchanged between your account and people who message you, to display your inbox and let you reply. Your Instagram access token is stored encrypted at rest, and message content is cached solely to show your conversations in the app. We use this data only to provide the messaging feature — we never use it for advertising, we never sell it, and we never share it with third parties. You can disconnect Instagram at any time from Profile → Instagram DMs, which deletes your stored access token and stops all further access; cached conversations are removed when you disconnect or delete your account. Our use of Instagram data complies with Meta’s Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

14. Your Rights

Under the NDPA you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent. Use Profile to update your details, or contact privacy@kashbook.app for requests we cannot fulfil in-app. Note that data we must keep for legal or compliance reasons (section 8) is exempt from deletion until its retention period ends.

15. Data Deletion

To delete your account and associated personal data, open Profile → Settings in the app, or email privacy@kashbook.app with the subject “Delete my data”. We will action your request subject to the legal-retention exceptions in section 8. Disconnecting Instagram (section 13) immediately removes your Instagram access token and cached messages.

16. Children’s Privacy

KashBook is intended for business owners and is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

17. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you within the app when we make significant changes. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.

18. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact privacy@kashbook.app. For anything else, support@kashbook.app or support@relianttechnology.com.ng.
